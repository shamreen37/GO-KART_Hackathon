GO KART RACING SAFETY

Go kart racing safety rules to follow to maximize your experience on our track:

  1. No bumping of the karts is allowed!!
  2. Avoid bumping into the barriers or anything else on the track.
  3. Do not stop on the track.
  4. A minimum height of 48” is required for youth drivers and 56” for adults.
  5. Keep both hands on the wheel at all times.
  6. Helmet, neck brace, seatbelt, and closed toe shoes are mandatory while racing. No high heels!
  7. All racers must attend a safety briefing on rules and regulations.
  8. Anyone under the influence of drugs or alcohol will not race. There is also no smoking in the facility.
